Earthquakes

An earthquake is a sudden and violent shaking of the ground caused by movement between tectonic plates.

    Before an earthquake:

  • Know your emergency evacuation routes and exits.
  • Secure heavy furniture and objects to walls.
  • Store flammable liquids and gases outside.
  • Have a fire extinguisher and know how to use it.
  • Practice earthquake drills with your family.


    • During an earthquake:

  • Drop to the ground and take cover under a steady piece of furniture.
  • Hold onto a firm furniture to prevent being knocked over.
  • Stay away from windows, mirrors, and glass doors.
  • Stay indoors until the tremors stops.
  • Do not run or stand in outdoors.
  • Don't panic


    • After an earthquake:

  • Inspect for injuries
  • Inspect for gas and water leaks.
  • Scrutinize for structural damage to your home.
  • Relocate/vacate from a place if instructed to do so ,by authorities.
  • Keep yourself abreast about earthquake risks and hotspots in your area.
  • Have a disaster supply kit which consists of all the basic necessities like food, water, and first aid supplies.
  • Consider installing earthquake-resistant fasteners on heavy furniture and objects.
  • Stay calm,composed and follow evacuation instructions from the authorities.

    • Remember!! preparation and knowledge are key to staying safe during an earthquake!
